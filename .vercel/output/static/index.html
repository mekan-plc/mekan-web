About Us

Your Technical Partner for World-Class Agribusiness

Based in Addis Ababa, Mekan Quality Management and Training PLC is a leading consultancy committed to connecting Ethiopian agribusinesses with global markets. The firm focuses on strengthening agricultural value chains through technical expertise, regulatory insight, and forward-looking strategy.

As an integrated technical partner, Mekan supports enterprises in meeting complex international requirements—ranging from EU market access and Global G.A.P. certification to ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) standards. By combining agronomic expertise with data-driven business strategy, the company enables clients to enhance value chain performance and achieve long-term, sustainable growth.
